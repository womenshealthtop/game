About the Memory Test Game

The Memory Test is a fun and engaging game designed to enhance your memory and cognitive abilities. Test how well you can remember patterns and improve your focus with every level!

How to Play

  • At the start of each level, a grid will display yellow tiles for 2 seconds.
  • Your task is to remember the positions of the yellow tiles.
  • Click on the tiles that were yellow to proceed to the next level.
  • Each level gets progressively harder with more tiles to remember.
  • Choose wrong tiles, and the game ends!

Gameplay and Scoring

In the Memory Test, your score increases as you pass each level. The faster you complete a level, the higher your score! Stay focused and challenge your memory with increasing difficulty.
